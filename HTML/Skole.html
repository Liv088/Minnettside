Food

Ubiquitous Chip (Ashton Lane, West End)


Located in the West End, Ubiquitous Chip is an iconic Glasgow restaurant known for its creative take on Scottish cuisine using fresh, local ingredients. The luxurious interior and courtyard setting make it perfect for signature dishes like venison, haggis with neeps and tatties, and locally sourced seafood.



Hiking

Conic Hill (4.2 miles (6.8 km) round trip)








Difficulty: Moderate

Duration: 2-3 hours

Conic Hill is only a 40-minute drive from Glasgow, and it offers a relatively short hike with rewarding views of Loch Lomond and its many islands. It's a part of the West Highland Way, and from the top you get a panoramic view of the Highland Boundary Fault, which separates the Highlands from the Lowlands.
